Pediatric (Kids) Dentist & General Dentist

Welcome to Smile Nation! Led by Dr. Komal Waghole, our clinic specializes in both general dentistry for adults and specialized pediatric care for children. We understand that visiting the dentist can be intimidating, which is why we focus on creating a comfortable, pain-free, and friendly environment.


With deep expertise as a kids' dentist, Dr. Komal ensures that your child's first dental visits are positive, laying the foundation for a lifetime of healthy oral habits. Whether it's a routine check-up, cavity prevention, or comprehensive adult dental care, you are in safe, expert hands.
