Fun Tooth Facts!

  • Super Strength: Your tooth enamel is the hardest part of your entire body—even harder than your bones!
  • Unique Smiles: Just like your fingerprints, your teeth are completely unique to you. No one else has the exact same smile!
  • Spit Power: Saliva is your mouth's superhero. It washes away food and protects your teeth from bad bacteria.

Myth vs. Reality 🕵️‍♂️

Myth: Sugar is the only thing that causes cavities.

Reality: Don't panic over an occasional sweet treat! Cavities are actually caused by the bacteria in your mouth. They eat leftover sugars AND carbs (like chips and bread) to make acid. Brushing washes that acid away!

Myth: Baby teeth don't matter since they fall out anyway.

Reality: Baby teeth are super important! They act as "space savers" for adult teeth. If they fall out too early from cavities, adult teeth might come in crooked. So, take good care of them!

Brush 2 times a day to keep the cavity bugs away! 🐛🚫
